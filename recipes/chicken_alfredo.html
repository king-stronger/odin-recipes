Chicken Alfredo

Description

Chicken Alfredo is a rich and creamy pasta dish that combines tender chicken pieces with a luscious Alfredo sauce. The sauce is made from a blend of butter, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese, creating a velvety texture that clings beautifully to the pasta. This dish is a favorite for its indulgent flavor and comforting qualities

Typically served over fettuccine, Chicken Alfredo can be enhanced with a sprinkle of freshly ground black pepper and a garnish of chopped parsley. Itâ€™s perfect for a cozy dinner at home or for impressing guests with a hearty, satisfying meal

Ingredients

  • Fettuccine pasta
  • Chicken breasts, sliced
  • Olive oil
  • Butter
  • Heavy cream
  • Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Garlic, minced
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the Pasta

    Cook fettuccine according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water and drain the rest

  2. Cook the chicken

    In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add sliced chicken breasts, season with salt and pepper, and cook until golden and cooked through. Remove from the skillet and set aside

  3. Prepare the Alfredo Sauce

    In the same skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic and cook for 1-2 minutes until fragrant. Pour in heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Stir in grated Parmesan cheese and cook until the sauce thickens, stirring frequently

  4. Combine Pasta and Sauce

    Add the cooked fettuccine to the skillet with the Alfredo sauce. Toss to coat the pasta evenly. If the sauce is too thick, add reserved pasta water a little at a time until desired consistency is reached. Add the cooked chicken back to the skillet and mix well

  5. Serve

    Divide the pasta among plates and top with freshly ground black pepper and chopped parsley, if desired
