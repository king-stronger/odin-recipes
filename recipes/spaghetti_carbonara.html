Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish that combines the creamy richness of eggs and cheese with the savory flavor of pancetta or bacon. This dish is known for its simplicity and quick preparation, making it a favorite for both weeknight dinners and special occasions. The sauce is made by whisking eggs and Parmesan cheese together, which then coats the hot pasta, creating a silky, luxurious texture

A generous sprinkle of black pepper adds a touch of spice, balancing the flavors perfectly. The crispy pancetta or bacon adds a delightful crunch and a burst of umami, making every bite a delightful experience. Whether served with a side salad or simply on its own, Spaghetti Carbonara is a comforting and satisfying meal

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Pancetta or bacon, diced
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Black pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic, minced (optional)
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cook the Pasta

    Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water and drain the rest.

  2. Cook the Pancetta

    In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add diced pancetta or bacon and cook until crispy. If using, add minced garlic and cook for an additional minute

  3. Prepare the Sauce

    In a bowl, whisk together eggs and grated Parmesan cheese until well combined. Season with black pepper

  4. Combine Pasta and Sauce

    Add the cooked spaghetti to the skillet with pancetta. Remove from heat and quickly pour in the egg mixture, tossing to coat the pasta. Add reserved pasta water a little at a time until the sauce reaches your desired consistency

  5. Serve

    Divide the pasta among plates and top with extra Parmesan cheese and black pepper
