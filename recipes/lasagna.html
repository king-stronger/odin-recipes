Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish known for its comforting and hearty layers. It features flat pasta sheets, rich meat sauce, and a blend of cheeses. The dish is typically prepared by alternating layers of lasagna noodles with a flavorful Bolognese sauce made from ground beef, tomatoes, onions, and garlic. This is then combined with a creamy mixture of ricotta and mozzarella cheese, creating a perfect harmony of textures and flavors

Baked to a golden, bubbly perfection, lasagna is a beloved family favorite and a staple at gatherings. The top layer is often finished with extra cheese, which melts and forms a delicious, crispy crust. Whether enjoyed fresh out of the oven or as leftovers, lasagna remains a go-to comfort food that brings warmth and satisfaction to any meal.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Olive oil
  • Onion, chopped
  • Garlic, minced
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Egg
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven

    Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C)

  2. Cook the Meat

    In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add chopped onion and minced garlic, cooking until fragrant. Add ground beef, season with salt and pepper, and cook until browned.

  3. Prepare the Sauce

    Stir in tomato sauce, let it simmer for 10 minutes

  4. Mix the Cheese Filling

    In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, egg, half of the shredded mozzarella, and half of the grated Parmesan. Season with a pinch of salt and pepper

  5. Assemble the Lasagna

    In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce. Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce. Spread a layer of the cheese mixture over the noodles, followed by another layer of meat sauce. Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of meat sauce

  6. Top with Cheese

    Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top

  7. Bake

    Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the top is golden and bubbly.

  8. Serve

    Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving
